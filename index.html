About

Ahmed Rangel, also known as 'Bayza'; was born and raised in La Chorrera, Panama in June 1998. He uses FL Studio for his music production.

During his career, he has been adopting genres such as Electro House, Trap, Dubstep, Melbourne Bounce, Minimal, Trance, Psytrance, Deep House and some Oriental vibes.

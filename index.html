Biography

🔷Ahmed Rangel, also known as 'Bayza'; was born and raised in La Chorrera, Panama 🇵🇦 in June 1998. He uses FL Studio for his music production.

🔷Significant Life Events:
⬜️ 2012, Started making reggae, hip-hop tracks just for fun.
⬜️ 2014, he decided to start making 'Electro House' music, in the 'Big Room' subgenre under the alias "Bayza", that is a variation of his maternal surname 'Baysa'.
    • In March, he self-released his first track on SoundCloud: "Blast Off".
     • In June, he signed and released with "Cube Cherry Records" his firsts tracks on Spotify: Collapse (June), Unleashe (July) and some more.
⬜️ 2016, in August he self-released a track for first time on Spotify: 'Audio Killer'.
⬜️ 2018, he inclined his interests in the music production of his tracks toward the genres Melbourne Bounce/Psytrance. He signed with 'Nelation' and first released: 'Convergence' (April).
⬜️ 2019, in September he self-released his first album: 'Genuine', it contains 13 tracks and most of them are Deep House

🔹During his career, he has been adopting genres such as Hardstyle, Trap, Dubstep, Melbourne Bounce, Minimal, Trance, Psytrance, Deep House and some Oriental vibes.
